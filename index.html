Bio

David Balik (TAAVO) is a DJ and producer from the Czech Republic known for his Deep and Minimal vibes surrounded by groovy melodies in contrast with classical instruments.

After his genre transformation from chilled tropical house to more deep and minimal techno style in the late of 2017, he started to work on his new Observation EP. The EP was released in June 2018 and consists of four melancholic and emotive singles, characterized by combination of minimalist elements and massive beats. Songs are closely associated with nature, which is captured in various ways in each song and links the entire EP.

He ended the year 2018 strong with the release of the single “Moonlight”. The song is defined again by massive beat with emotional background where you can feel the sound of every element complete with a never-ending uplifting melody of synthesizers. TAAVO’s emotional mix of techno, ambient and deep house takes you on a journey like no other.

At the begining of 2020, TAAVO released his second EP called “Hibernation”. It opens with celestial intro “Diversity”, which is the only song without a beat. The following songs “Hibernation”, “Reborn” and “Empathy” lead listeners more to the dancefloor, because of the beat and fluttering synths. The entire EP is characterized by atmospheric harmonies and melodies that are constantly escalating. These synths elements together with crisscrossing layers of percussion, vocal's chops and lush harmonies as strings and arpeggio are connected across the whole EP.
