Bio

TAAVO (David Balik) is 22 years old Czech producer and DJ. Since young age he has been playing piano and guitar. He fell in love with composing music at the age 14 and his first remix went out in January 2015. Up to now he has released 8 remixes and 6 original songs, where he worked together with international singers. People love his music because of his chill out positive vibes and the feelings which he can spread through his music.
